The Quest For a Single Number

We often try to compare players based on single metrics - statistics such as wins/goals above replacement, game score, points per game, and expected goals (amongst many others). However, these statistics are typically heavily correlated which suggests that they are possibly the result of common, unobservable factors. This idea serves as the basis for this entire web application!
